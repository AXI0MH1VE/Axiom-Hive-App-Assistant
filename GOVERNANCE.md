# Governance

This document describes governance principles for the Axiom Hive project.

Owner and authority

- The repository owner and project steward is Nicholas Michael Grossi. He is the authoritative decision-maker for project direction, licensing, and public disclosure.

Decision process

- Decisions about public-facing policy, licensing, and acceptance of major architectural changes rest with the owner.
- Minor fixes and documentation edits may be accepted via pull request subject to owner review.

Security and incident response

- Security reports follow the process in `SECURITY.md`.
- The owner maintains final authority on disclosure timing and remediation priorities.

Contribution review

- Contributions are reviewed for technical correctness, adherence to the framework's principles, and preservation of attribution.

Transparency and audit

- Important decisions, releases, and security advisories will be recorded in the repository's release notes and `CHANGELOG.md`.
